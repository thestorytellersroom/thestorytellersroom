ABOUT THE FOUNDER

  I, Deba(Debobrata Patra), am the Founder to The Storytellers' room. I am an IT engineering student of Netaji Subhash Engineering College.

  Basically, I'm a musician, photographer by hobby. My work in the field of audio mastering started from this family only. In the beginning, I thought only about the students of boards examination who would learn fast by hearing these stories, but later I decided to do something for the everyone.

  The Storytellers' room(STR) is not just a storytelling group, it's just like my second family. A place where an auspicious members are gifted.

  I hope it serves a great purpose and reaches out to people and attains success in entertaining them.
