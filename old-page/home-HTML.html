CHANNEL DESCRIPTION

  'Story', this word depicts every human's deep emotion. In this world each and every elements have their own stories to tell. We just need to have a mindset to explore, sense to understand each and every letter that a storyteller is telling us. When we read some story on our own we have a limited fun, but when we are listening that story from a storyteller as if all the characters is awakening in our senses and talking to us. It is as same as in our childhood when our grandparents were telling us stories with funny, scary and lots of expression just like in the book.

  In "The Storytellers' Room" you will get to relive and experience that same thriiling. Do not hear story only with ears but listen to it with your heart. Feel every moment, learn from every character.
